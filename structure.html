1. Board of Directors

The Board of Directors provides overall governance, oversight, strategic direction, and accountability. It ensures compliance with the organization’s mission, vision, and values.

Key Roles:

  • Board Chairperson
  • Vice Chairperson
  • Treasurer
  • Secretary
  • Board Members (representatives from faith-based, community development, legal, finance, and education sectors)

2. Management Team (Executive Leadership)

Led by the Executive Director, the Management Team is responsible for daily operations, strategic implementation, and leadership of the ministry’s five pillars.

  • Executive Director: Overall leadership and coordination of LBEM programs and strategy. Reports to the Board of Directors.
  • Programs and Business Director: Oversees program development, implementation, and income-generating activities. Supervises pillar coordinators and ensures operational effectiveness.
  • Children’s Ministry Coordinator: Oversees discipleship and welfare programs for children and youth. Coordinates with churches, schools, and community-based partners.
  • Theological Education & Discipleship Coordinator: Manages all residential and satellite theological training and discipleship programs. Ensures doctrinal soundness and spiritual formation of participants.
  • Skills Development & Training Coordinator: Leads planning and execution of vocational and business skills training. Builds partnerships with skills training institutions and local businesses.
  • Advocacy & Rights Coordinator: Champions social justice, legal aid, and advocacy for vulnerable groups. Liaises with government, civil society, and legal networks.
  • Psycho-social Support and Welfare Coordinator: Coordinates counseling services and social support programs. Supports families, trauma survivors, and vulnerable individuals.
  • Finance and Administration Manager: Manages budgeting, accounting, audits, HR, and procurement. Ensures compliance with policies and financial integrity.
  • Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEL) Officer: Tracks program effectiveness, impact, and learning for sustainability. Supports evidence-based decision-making and reporting.

3. Support Staff

Reporting to respective Coordinators or Managers:

  • Project Officers
  • Counselors
  • Trainers/Facilitators
  • Administrative Assistants
  • Field Officers
  • Volunteers
