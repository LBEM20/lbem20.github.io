Organizational Description

Leadership Building and Empowerment Ministry (LBEM) is a faith-based, non-profit organization committed to transforming lives through holistic empowerment. Rooted in Christian values, LBEM operates through five foundational pillars aimed at equipping individuals, strengthening communities, and fostering spiritual and socio-economic resilience. Through discipleship, theological education, skills development, advocacy, and psychosocial support, LBEM seeks to raise a generation of servant leaders who are spiritually grounded, socially aware, and economically empowered.

  4. Advocacy: Championing the rights and dignity of vulnerable populations, promoting justice, social equity, and good governance based on biblical principles.
